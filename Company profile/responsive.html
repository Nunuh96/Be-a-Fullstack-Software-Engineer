Introduction

Being a "superior person" can describe someone with superior qualities, like wisdom, skill, and compassion, who seeks personal growth and treats others with respect and dignity. Alternatively, the term can refer to a superiority complex, a psychological state marked by an outwardly confident but inwardly insecure attitude, often leading to arrogance, a need for control, and a difficulty accepting criticism.

Quality of a Truly Superior Person

  1. Personal growth and self awereness
  2. Integrity and Compassion
  3. Humality and Objectivity
  4. Emotional maturity
  5. Action oriented and Visionary
  6. Contribution to others
